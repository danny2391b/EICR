Common EICR questions.

Fast answers before you book.

How long does an EICR take?

Most flats take around 1-2 hours. Larger homes and commercial properties can take longer depending on circuits, access, and installation condition.

What happens if the property fails?

The report will list the observation codes and recommended remedial work. You can arrange repairs separately or ask us for an itemised quote.

Do landlords need an EICR?

Landlords in England generally need electrical installations inspected and tested at least every five years, and must provide reports when required.

Can tenants or agents arrange access?

Yes. Add access notes during booking and we can coordinate appointment details with tenants, agents, key safes, or site contacts.
